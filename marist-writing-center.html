Marist Writing Center

Marketing Assistant
Higher Education / Instagram, TikTok / Social Media & PR Team / Student Engagement

Project Overview

The Marist Writing Center provides tutoring support for students working on academic writing assignments, including drafting, revising, and proofreading. Social media is used to promote Writing Center services and encourage students to schedule tutoring appointments throughout the academic year.

My Role

As a member of the Writing Center's Social Media and PR team, I helped develop and execute engaging content for Instagram and TikTok, with the goal of increasing awareness of tutoring services and connecting with students across campus.

Scope of Work

  • Created TikTok and Instagram content built around campus moments and trend-based formats
  • Designed promotional graphics and posters using Canva
  • Promoted campus writing events and tutoring services through short-form video
  • Used trend-based content to grow reach and increase student engagement

Results

245K+
Views on top TikTok video
40K+
Views on additional content
24.5K+
Total TikTok likes

Trend-based content drove significantly higher engagement, with one short-form TikTok comparing AI tools to in-person tutoring reaching 245K+ views and 21K likes. The campaign supported steady growth across both Instagram (784 followers) and TikTok (487 followers) and increased overall student awareness of Writing Center services.
