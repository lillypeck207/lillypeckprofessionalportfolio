About · 01

Hi, I'm Lilly. Nice to meet you.

I'm a marketer who genuinely loves the work. Equal parts storyteller, strategist, and project coordinator, with a soft spot for analytics dashboards and a well organized content calendar.

I'm a senior at Marist University, graduating in May 2026 with a B.S. in Marketing and a minor in Advertising. My time on campus and through internships has given me the chance to do real work. I've built social strategies from scratch, written copy that actually had to convert, kept multi client schedules on track, and watched the numbers respond (or not) the next morning.

Across roles at Morning Ag Clips, the Marist Writing Center, BLKDOG Designs, and Access Anvil Corp., I've planned content calendars, produced photo and video, written email and blog copy, coordinated campaigns across teams, and helped roll out new brand identities. One of my TikToks pulled 245K+ views, and one of my client campaigns pushed monthly reach up 1,100% during peak season. I learned quickly that great marketing happens at the intersection of creativity, careful coordination, and curiosity about what the data is telling you.

What I care about: campaigns that feel like they were made by a human being, brands that know exactly who they are, and the kind of small, specific details that make people stop scrolling. I'm open to a wide range of opportunities, including digital marketing, project management or coordination, marketing assistant roles, scheduling and operations, copywriting, and content strategy. I'm looking for a team that values thoughtful work, moves with intention, and isn't afraid to do something a little different.
