Access Anvil Corp.

Marketing & Brand Strategy Intern
Construction & Manufacturing / Greenwich, NY / Brand Launch / Internal & External Communications

Project Overview

Access Anvil Corp. is a construction and manufacturing company based in Greenwich, New York, specializing in commercial fencing, concrete forming, and fabrication. It operates under the umbrella of The Fort Miller Group Inc., alongside several sister companies. During my internship, the company was undergoing a brand refresh, and I supported marketing initiatives by creating content and contributing to a strategic plan for launching the company's new logo and refreshed brand identity.

My Role

I supported marketing communications and social media content while contributing to a strategic launch plan for the company's new logo and refreshed brand identity. I also created internal communication materials shared across Access Anvil and its sister companies under The Fort Miller Group.

Scope of Work

  • Created and managed social media content for the company's Facebook and LinkedIn accounts
  • Designed banners, posters, and presentation graphics for company communications
  • Developed a multi-phase logo launch strategy outlining internal communication, social media rollout, and brand integration
  • Designed internal communication posters distributed across sister companies under The Fort Miller Group
  • Planned customer-feedback surveys to measure brand perception during the rollout
  • Attended internal meetings with team members and partner companies to gain insight into marketing strategy and business operations

Results

The work produced a structured framework for the brand rollout, improved coordination of internal communication across The Fort Miller Group's family of companies, and gave the marketing team a clear, multi-phase plan for launching the new logo to customers and employees with a consistent message.
