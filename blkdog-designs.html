BLKDOG Designs

Copy & Content Intern
Digital Marketing Agency / Email & Blog Copywriting / Mailchimp / Multi-Client Campaigns

Project Overview

BLKDOG Designs is a digital marketing and branding agency that supports a wide range of businesses through content strategy, social media management, and online brand growth. During my internship, I contributed to multiple client campaigns by creating written content, scheduling posts, conducting research, and helping document case studies.

My Role

I supported digital marketing efforts for several client accounts through content creation, research, and ongoing campaign support, working across email marketing, social media, and brand-storytelling deliverables.

Scope of Work

  • Wrote blog and email marketing content for client campaigns
  • Built and scheduled email campaigns in Mailchimp
  • Scheduled social media posts across multiple platforms for several clients
  • Conducted research on companies and industries to support marketing content
  • Created written case studies highlighting client work and campaign outcomes
  • Identified backlinks and SEO opportunities for client websites

Results

The work I contributed helped strengthen brand storytelling across several client accounts and improved content consistency on social platforms. Email campaigns supported product launches and community engagement initiatives, while research-driven case studies gave the agency reusable proof points for future client conversations.
